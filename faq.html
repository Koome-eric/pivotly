What is Go Highlevel?

Go Highlevel is a comprehensive platform designed for marketing and sales agencies to streamline their operations and boost their productivity. It's an all-in-one solution that combines various tools to help businesses manage their clients, leads, campaigns, appointments, and more.

What integrations does Go Highlevel support?

Go Highlevel supports several integrations to help businesses streamline their workflows and increase their productivity. Some of the most popular integrations include:

  1. Zapier
    Go Highlevel integrates with Zapier, allowing businesses to connect their workflows with over 2,000 other apps, such as Google Sheets, Trello, and Asana.
  2. Stripe:
    Go Highlevel integrates with Stripe, allowing businesses to accept payments directly from the platform.
  3. Twilio:
    Go Highlevel integrates with Twilio, enabling businesses to send and receive SMS messages directly from the platform.

How much does Go Highlevel cost?

Go Highlevel offers different pricing plans to cater to businesses of all sizes. The plans include:

  1. Standard:
    The standard plan costs $97 per month and includes access to all the essential features of the platform.
  2. Pro:
    The pro plan costs $297 per month and includes additional features such as white-labeled reporting, custom domains, and more.
  3. Enterprise:
    The enterprise plan is customized to fit specific business needs and includes features such as dedicated support and advanced reporting.

What is Pivotly?

Pivotly is a comprehensive e-commerce solution that empowers businesses to optimize their online store for maximum performance. It provides a wide range of features and tools that enable businesses to customize their online store, improve their customer experience, and streamline their operational processes.

Pivotly offers a range of features and tools that can help businesses optimize their online store. These include advanced product options, custom fields, dynamic pricing, and custom checkout fields. Pivotly also offers a range of marketing tools like abandoned cart recovery, email marketing, and social media integration.
